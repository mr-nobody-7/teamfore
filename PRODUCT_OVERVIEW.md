# Product Overview

## Vision

TeamFore gives organizations a single source of truth for leave planning, workforce availability, and team capacity.

## Core problems solved

- Leave requests and approvals are often fragmented across chat, email, and spreadsheets.
- Managers lack immediate visibility into team capacity impact before approving leave.
- Teams need holiday-aware and status-aware scheduling to reduce delivery risk.

## Primary users

- **User**: applies for leave, tracks status, updates availability/workload.
- **Manager**: approves/rejects leave, views team planning signals and reports.l
- **Admin**: workspace-wide visibility and configuration (teams, users, settings, audit logs).

## Value proposition

- Faster approvals with better context
- Fewer staffing surprises
- More transparent team coordination
- Better historical insights for planning

## Key workflows

1. User submits leave request.
2. System checks overlap/session validity and computes warning context.
3. Manager/Admin reviews pending requests with smart capacity warning when needed.
4. Team calendar reflects approved leave and capacity heatmap.
5. Reports and dashboard summarize usage, capacity, and trends.

## Product pillars

- **Clarity**: clean status views and role-focused interfaces.
- **Safety**: workspace isolation, role-based access, audit trails.
- **Planning intelligence**: warning thresholds, heatmap risk signals, analytics.
